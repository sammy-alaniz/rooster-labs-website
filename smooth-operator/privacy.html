Smooth Operator

Privacy Policy

about support privacy

effective: march 14, 2026

overview

Smooth Operator ("the App") is developed by Rooster Labs. This policy explains what data the App collects, which is none.

data collection

Smooth Operator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App runs entirely on your device. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts.

data storage

All mission data you create is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to any of your files or data.

third-party services

The App uses Apple MapKit to display maps. Map tile requests are handled by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. The mission library uses iCloud containers to sync your missions across your devices. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is not accessible to Rooster Labs. Smooth Operator does not send any additional data to third parties.
