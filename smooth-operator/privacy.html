Privacy Policy

about support privacy

effective: march 14, 2026

Smooth Operator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no accounts. Mission data is stored locally on your device and synced across your devices via iCloud containers in your personal iCloud account, which Rooster Labs cannot access. The app uses Apple MapKit for maps, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
